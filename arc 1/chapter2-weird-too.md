# Chapter 2 - Weird too

He's about a year older than me, but why does it feels like he's way older?

My first week here, im pretty sure i saw him with the hammer at the workshop.. I thought the teacher said that we would only be in the bellows. Even the older kids didn't held the hammer. Maybe its because he is on the bigger side. I dont mean he's big, like big big, but big like big… ugh nvm. 

Other boys seems to respect him and girls seems to like him. He always smiles and talk to others. How did he even find topics to talk about? I shall take a knife and dissect that brain of his.. Maybe i can take a thing or too for myself so i will always know what is the right thing to say. 

Its been a month since ive been here. I like it here. It is calm, the other kids seems to not be bothering me which means that im not a nuisance. Or maybe I am and they are just avoiding me. But they did smile when they saw me so maybe not.. or are they just pretending? I hope not. 

After doing my morning reading at the library, i rushed to the fields as it nearing 11 and the instructor will start being noisy. I really hope im not too late to the point they'll notice me. Im really scared of them. I feel like one wrong move can get me pretty badly beaten up by them so i avoid putting myself in that position in the first place. I get into line and immediately made a mistake. 

Am i too close to this guy. Oh no.. what do i do? Ok lets stay still and play dead.. play dead Lily play dead. Oh no is he starting at me? Why is he just staring at me.. please i didnt mean to stand too close.. please forgive me and stop staring at me. Look front, look front… phew he turned. That's pretty weird. He stared at me for quite a while. Thank god. I glanced to see who the guy is..

Oh no. Its him. I just realized that it is that guy. The hammer guy. Im dead. He thinks im weird now. He'll use his influence to make the whole orphanage against me, then all of them will hate me, then I'll be sold away or worse killed! Oh no forgive me.. what should i do.. Its ok stay calm. You'll apologise later. Hmm but what if ill say something that'll make it even worse. 
A flashback came in.. There were people in the house. Loud. Father knelt down to my height and held my face. His hands were shaking.

Shh Lily. Whatever they ask you.. just shut your mouth. Please. Dont say a thing.

Yes father.

They took him through the door. Right before it closed

I love you Lily. 

Then the door.

Yeah, lets not say anything to him. You'll never know what'll happen.. I cant remember much before the orphanage. Amnesia probably. In one of the stories i read, the boy fell down and hit his head and lost his memory. I remembered falling once, first week i was here. I bled quite a bit actually. Maybe it happened then. I tried falling again on purpose to see if the memories came back but it just hurt and the bleeding was worse that time and the memories stayed the same so probably not that.

I didn't tell the teachers about the bleeding. I mean what would i say. And what if they got angry. Better to just wait for it to stop.

Im a good bellower. I mean its just up and down, and just make sure the fire is as big as the smith wants. Its not that hard. Its easy as well. It looks like my effort to train every morning is paying off. Im lazy most of the time and I would rather read books. But you never when time calls for an escape and when that time comes, i need to be prepared to be the fastest. I also do include some body training because i feel lighter doing so, resulting into me being faster. I really want to race somebody but what if they dont want to? But its ok for now ill just train for my own betterment.

While bellowing also the smith is really intimidating. He kept asking questions i dont know about. What does he want me to say? How do i answer "How long do i want to work for?" I mean as long as i need to, but what if he's angry at the answer because i dont want to help him? He's putting me in a tight spot and im scared honestly and i want to go back and continue my reading at the library. The library is uncomfortable anyway, but it is forbidden for me to take the book away, i don't want to get caught. 

Finally the shift ends and the smith thanked me. I am getting quite hungry so i make my way to the dining hall. I dont really eat much, just in case anyone else wants a bigger portion they can take mine. I really dont want to be seen taking too many food. Hopefully i took just enough so others can eat too. I sat down at my regular seat and start eating. I thought about my interactions with the smith just now. He is really talkative. He told stories about his family, his cat and the rabbit of Warren. Apparently people can turn into rabbits here because the village legend is a rabbit. There are a lot of rabbit stories but the one I remembered is the rabbit that tricked crocodiles. I think the rabbit is stupid at the end and is just lucky that the deity is there. 

Suddenly, i saw a familiar face. The girl that i always sat across to.. why is her on the other row. Wait.. oh no. This is not my regular place. Ok calm down. Its ok. Maybe the owner didnt come today. 

"Hey.."

Chills ran down my spine.. its that guy isnt it.

WHO ARE YOU?.. he asked yeah im dead. He is going to hammer me down with that adult strength of his. I need to get out of here… Ok i think ill just go before he acts. So i stood up.

"SIT DOWN!"

I sat right back down. Omg im so scared. He is looking straight through me. Im dead. Seriously im dead. He looks like he's about to eat me. 

"WHERE ARE YOU FROM!"

Yeah im dead. Im playing dead now. I try my best to just look at him, hopefully he decides im not worth the prey.

"WHATS YOUR NAME!"
"HOW OLD ARE YOU!"

He is preparing to pick on me. That is why he is asking for my name. Oh no.. why  Lily why why why did you have to sit here out of all places. That damn smith talking too much. Its not his fault but.. ughh this is so annoying. 

After a while, he finally stopped. He starts eating. Thank god. I was about to past out. Its ok. At least he's eating his food not me… This is intense. Its like he's preparing to do something but i dont know what. Is he going to jump out and eat me next? Im so scared.

He's so scary to the point that im scared to eat. So he finishes first. Ah crap, he's staring at me again. He suddenly smiles. Not like a happy smile, but the smile i give when i look at a baby.. why is he smiling like that. He opened his mouth 

Ok since you dont want to talk ill just say what i want to say… then he proceeds to say the most beautiful thing someone has ever said to me… he watches me? Why? Why? Why? Why and how did he know i wake up early? He knows i go to the library and read? I think im good with the teachers and the older kids seems to not bother me, but he watches that too? Why does he care? Why me? I have so many questions to him. I wanted to cry honestly.

Yeah, he said he cared about me.. why? What did i do to him? I know im good looking but is that enough to warrant that much attention from him? Does he wants something from me. I have a little bit of bread left.. hmm but i dont think so. I dont have anything going on for me so again.. why?

He smiles one last time and got up, holding his tray. "Wait", i said to him..

MY VOICE CRACKED! Omg i will die of embarrassment. My voice cracked, That is soo uncool.. He must think im weird or something…Yeah he laughed. Ill go cry now. 

"No no what do you want to say?" He asked

".. Lily, my name is Lily." Im trying to sound cool but i really am crying inside.

You can talk?

"What do you mean? Of course i can. Weird thing to ask, im 8, not 3." Ok im 8, i really rake offend to that. What do you mean 'you can talk?'

"You do know that its been a month since you've been here, this is the first time ever anyone had heard your voice." He said seriously.

… crap.. really. Nobody's heard my voice. Crap.. really, i must've said something, many people talked to me, the smith, the teachers the libra… omg i didnt say anything back. Oh no. Why didn't i say anything back? 

"I know, i chose not to". No way im telling him that i forgot to talk. "I prefer not to talk". CRAP REALLY? I forgot to talk to people. Im toast. What do i do..

"Alright, apart from the crack you sounds nice."He said while laughing. Yeah he got a death wish. But he still can eat me if he wants to so i'll let that slide.

Yeah im Rowan, nice to finally speak with you Lily. 

Rowan.. nice name. I thought to myself.
