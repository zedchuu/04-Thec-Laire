# Chapter 1 - Weird

she's pretty..
But she is kinda weird. I mean its been a month since she came here and i havent heard her speak, or even make a sound at all. She barely ate every meal. She woke up, read, exercise, do chores and thats it. No playing no nothing. Can she continue on living like this? I know i cant.

Ive been here for 8 years. Many students of the orphanage comes and goes. Some are too weak so the orphanage 'transferred ' them to other places. Some awakened Warren's Shape, the rabbit, they went to another place, and lately is sold to buyers. This is what happened to Seriph. Btw, the village name is Warren, and Shape is a superpower.

"Oi you sons of pigs, hurry up dammit! I told you to be here by 11!"One of the teachers yelled…. Its only 10.50 calm down.

I groaned silently. Im one of the longest student there, so i knows how it goes. The teacher will stay stuff as if he told them beforehand and curse them out because the students didn't follow ( that teacher is just a bad person and enjoy cursing on others imo). So I got in line and prepare for my shift. My job today is blacksmithing so I will be in the workshop for at least until right before dinner which means it is a long day. 

There are a couple of jobs that the orphanage handle. Regular blacksmithing jobs by the castle, cleaning the city, constructions (helping around), wood work and laundry. All tedious stuff really. The heavy jobs will be assigned to us either by age 7 or when we look big enough. Unfortunately im the latter so I've been working since I was 5. Compared to my friends, I enjoy working. It gives me stuff to do and trains me in a way. Idk miss Elyana used to say that its not all bad and I find that true. I am getting stronger and when im older I'll definitely be the best worker and get paid the most.

I walked into the field where everyone is starting to gather. Im on the bigger side of the students. Even thought im only 9, i can pass as pre teen. Suddenly i feel a gust of wind hit my left side.

There are around 200 total students at the orphanage. We were divided in to groups of 10, and each group will rotate doing jobs that were assigned to the orphanage. So for tasks like laundry,  1 group is needed. For helping out constructions, 2 groups needed. So if you're lucky, you might just stay back in the orphanage, where after the orphanage is clean enough, the teachers couldn't care less what you're doing.

As i looked left, i shrieked. Since when is she beside me, and too close at that. I looked at her, waiting to get her attention so i can speak to her. Also good to introduce myself to her… she has been in my group for a month, but we never got paired together. So i never really got the chance to talk to her… any minute now.. Is she really not going to look at me? She's right beside me and im looking directly and obviously at her. 

Okay attention scoundrels! The teacher looked at our group. So I gave up and face the teacher. You're on bellows duty today.. except for Rowan, until dinner. keep the heat steady or you'll ruin the whole batch and you'll all starve a little hungrier. Go!

We scurried to our stations. I parted ways with her and watch her disappears at the corners. Weird girl really. I think she is a year younger than me. I heard that her family is killed which is traumatising to say the least. All i know is since she got here,.. not just me, but everyone including the teachers hasn't heard her say a word. Im kinda worried about her though. Because naturally people will ignore her once they are used to her not talking. Its been a month so she is relatively new. 

Also she reminds me of my late sister Seriph. She was 4 years older than me and we were inseparable since i can remember. She is the quiet type and sometimes a pain as she was so shy. She was very kind too to the point that she does more than she needs to. Miss Elyana used to scold her so much for this. 

Seriph used to just smile and say its ok. I used to think she meant that, before i caught her crying in the restroom. Since that i tell myself that i am her bodyguard. So whenever someone does something to Seriph, that i know they shouldn't, I'd stepped up and protect her. This of course led to be being beaten to a pulp a lot of times but thats ok, at least i've gotten stronger and Seriph seems happier. And i like it, even when im crying she just smiles and thanks me and showers me with compliments. She is very nice and a very caring sister i could ask for. 

3 years ago when i gotten back from a laundry job i heard she got awakened. Warren is a rabbit place, so that became her Shape. Miss Elyana told me that across the world, each place has different Shapes and shapes is passed on each generation. Some village have only 1 animal as shapes, some have many and some have none. Only chosen one gets to be shape. Some people also got what they call Resonance. It is basically resonances to the Shape. In the orphanage, there are a couple of older children that have resonances, some can jump high, some can be evasive and stealthy, some are quick. The Queen here is said to have resonance as well. She got 12 children so yeah that does make sense. 

So resonance is rare on its own, but shapes are way rarer, and Seriph got one. Unfortunately that is the time the current grandmaster, Prester came in and all hell breaks loose. Safe to say I hated him the most in this lifetime. He is the one most responsible to Seriph's passing and it is because he is money hungry and doesn't care about us. He fired ms elyana first thing he came saying she's expensive and sold off Seriph to the highest bidder. I dont know what happened after Seriph went off with the highest bidder, but a week later he came back wanting a refund saying she was dead. I remember walloping for a month a fighting anyone and everyone who tried to calm me down. Anyway thats a story for later.

"Wow that is very nice Rowan." Said Mr Pelo. Mr Pelo is the very talkative smith a couple of stations over. It seems he is paired with Lily which is ironic and funny. I just let out a little laugh at his compliment. 

I like blacksmithing. Im always on the stronger side so i can hit the metal relatively well. After years of experience i also can hit more precisely. Im proud to say that given more years i think i can open a shop of my own, especially after i get out of this orphanage. Of course, im one of only kids who can actually do the job, other kids are too weak to handle it. I think this is because of early exposure and the fights i had for Seriph.


Later at dinner, all students were exhausted (seems like everyone has gotten jobs). It is winter so the snow cleaning crew group is hit the hardest. After getting my food, i went to sit at my regular place. I usually eat alone, but not because i have no friends.. Well its because i have no friends. I may look fine, but the rate of students come and go really is high, either they died in some way, or as of lately being sold off. Miss Elyana always told me that for friendship it is better to have quality over quantity, but still be nice to others, and thats what i did. I had a total of 3 real friends, 2 of them, including Seriph is dead, and one is brought off. I really hope i can meet him again in the future.

Anyway as I was heading towards my seat, I saw her.. why is she in my seat. She normally sat on the other row. Nonetheless, finally, a chance to talk with her.

"Excuse me, did you know i always sit here?" I asked. 

 She blinked twice at me. She really is just staring into my face. Any minute now… oh no she got up.

"On no no, don't get up its ok im just messing with you." As i proceed to pull the chair next to her. She sat down promptly. Lets begin the interrogation session. I thought.

"So.. where are you from?…"
"What even is your name?…"
"How old are you again?…"

A minute pass… Yeah so she will not talk. She just stares at me and continues eating. Hmph fine i'll not bother her.

"Ok fine i get it you dont want to talk. Just so you know i do want to talk to you. So if you want to ask me anything go ahead. I'll leave you alone now." I said before start eating. I was hoping she know that i want to talk to her so maybe while i was eating she will talk to me. When i glimpse at her… she is just.. staring.. at me. Now i feel uncomfortable. Come on, say something! I ate quickly because somehow i felt embarrassed.

After im done, she was somewhat done. I turn my body facing her to say a couple things that I want to say to her. Its a couple of my observations of her that i dont know when to tell her if ever.

"Ok since you dont want to talk ill just say what i want to say. Firstly, you dont have to wake up at 5 everyday. You can sleep in and gather at 7 like the rest of us, you dont have to be early just because you're new. Secondly, you can borrow the book that you are reading at the library you know? You don't have to just read it at the library everytime. Just borrow it then when you're done return it. Then you can read during bedtime. Next don't ignore some of the teachers. I know there are a lot of bad teachers but the some of the teachers are nice and is willing to help you with most things if you asked. Oh and I know you dont care, but dont stick too long around the older kids, just in case they find it funny to make fun of you. I don't know what you've been through but I know that at a couple of the teachers is really worried about you. I worry too so i hope you can take care here." After seeing her staring at me like always, i take it as my cue to leave.

I think i said it in a proper way. Ms Elyana always reminded me that we dont know what others have been through so we must be careful with our words.

Well tomorrow its my team's shift to shovel out the thick snow and i really am not looking forward to it.

Then as i walk away, i hand reached out behind my back. Suddenly i heard the weirdest noise ive heard in a while.
